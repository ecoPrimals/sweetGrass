## 🌟 What Makes SweetGrass Special

### Fair Attribution
Automatically calculates fair credit for all contributors based on roles, derivation chains, and time decay. Enables automatic reward distribution for AI training, content licensing, and research collaboration.

### Complete Provenance
W3C PROV-O compliant provenance tracking with full DAG queries. Compatible with research repositories, government archives, and healthcare systems worldwide.

### Privacy by Design
GDPR-inspired privacy controls (5 levels) built in from day one. Not bolted on later.

### Capability-Based
Infant discovery pattern means primals start knowing only themselves and discover dependencies at runtime. True decentralization.

### Standards-First
W3C PROV-O, JSON-LD, DID standards support ensures global interoperability.
