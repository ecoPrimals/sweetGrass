## 💡 Key Innovations

### 1. **Fair Attribution Algorithm**
Automatically calculates fair credit for all contributors based on:
- Role weighting (12 types: Creator, Contributor, Reviewer, etc.)
- Derivation chain analysis
- Time decay factors
- Recursive attribution propagation

**Use Cases**: AI training rewards, content licensing, research collaboration

### 2. **Infant Discovery Pattern**
Zero-knowledge bootstrap architecture:
- Primals start knowing only themselves
- Discover dependencies at runtime via capabilities
- No hardcoded addresses or primal names
- Pure capability-based coordination

**Result**: True decentralization, no central configuration

### 3. **W3C PROV-O Compliance**
Native support for global provenance standard:
- JSON-LD export
- Compatible with research repositories
- Government archive ready
- Healthcare system compatible

**Result**: Global interoperability

### 4. **Zero-Copy Optimizations**
Minimizes allocations throughout:
- `Cow<str>` for strings
- `Arc` for shared data
- Borrowing over cloning
- Efficient serialization

**Result**: 8x performance gain in parallel operations

## 🌾 The Story of SweetGrass

SweetGrass is the attribution layer for the ecoPrimals ecosystem. It answers three fundamental questions:

1. **Where did this data come from?** (Provenance)
2. **Who contributed to it?** (Attribution)
3. **How should credit be distributed?** (Fair calculation)

In a world of AI training, content creation, and collaborative research, these questions matter more than ever. SweetGrass provides the infrastructure to answer them with:

- **Cryptographic guarantees** (content-addressable Braids)
- **Fair algorithms** (role-based attribution)
- **Global standards** (W3C PROV-O)
- **Privacy controls** (GDPR-inspired)
- **Zero trust** (capability-based discovery)
